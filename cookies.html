Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, mobile phone, or tablet) when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They include authentication cookies and preference cookies that remember your choices.

Performance Cookies

We use performance cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve site functionality. Tools like Google Analytics fall into this category.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance functionality by remembering your preferences (language, username, etc.) so you don't have to re-enter them.

Marketing Cookies

Third-party advertising partners may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing behavior across different websites.

Why We Use Cookies

  • To enable website functionality and user preferences
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To improve site performance and user experience
  • To serve relevant advertisements
  • To maintain your login session
  • To remember your settings and preferences

Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: To track visitor statistics and site performance
  • Advertising Partners: To display relevant ads and track ad performance
  • Social Media: Social sharing buttons may place cookies

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to control cookies on your device. Most web browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored and delete them
  • Block all cookies or only third-party cookies
  • Allow sites to set cookies but delete them when closing your browser
  • Block cookies from specific websites

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality. Some features might not work as intended if essential cookies are disabled.

Browser Settings Guide

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

GDPR & Cookie Consent

We respect user privacy and comply with GDPR regulations. We aim to obtain your explicit consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can withdraw your consent at any time.

Cookie Duration

Cookies have varying lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 1 year)

Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. We honor DNT signals where applicable, though not all advertisers do.

Updates to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology and privacy practices. We recommend reviewing this page regularly.
